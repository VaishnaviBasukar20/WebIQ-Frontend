About WebIQ

WebIQ is a cutting-edge AI-powered tool designed to streamline information retrieval from the web. By simply providing a URL, our intelligent system scrapes and analyzes the content, creating a comprehensive knowledge base from which it can answer your specific questions. Our mission is to transform how you interact with online information, making it faster, more efficient, and incredibly intuitive.

We are a team of passionate developers and AI enthusiasts dedicated to building powerful, user-friendly tools that leverage the latest in large language models and web technologies. We believe in providing clear, accurate, and context-rich answers, empowering you to make informed decisions and accelerate your research.

Privacy and Concerns

We take your privacy seriously. WebIQ only scrapes the content you explicitly provide a URL for. We do not track your browsing history or collect any personal data beyond what is necessary to operate the service. All data is processed securely and is not shared with any third parties.

The scraped data is stored temporarily to answer your questions and is deleted after a set period. We are committed to transparency and are continuously improving our practices to ensure your data remains safe and secure.
